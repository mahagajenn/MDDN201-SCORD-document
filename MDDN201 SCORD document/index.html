IDEA

Scord is a browser extension which essentially records the screen of your internet browser. As users interact with internet pages, Scord then translate the activities into an in-depth scan and tracks your internet usage. Accessible for users to learn and see their online activity, Scord informs users how the issue of data and internet privacy affects people.

IDEATION

For Assignment Two I have chosen to make a website prototype that shows how SCORD will function as an active website. Using softwares such as Adobe Xd and Nicepage, I will begin to make wireframes to get an idea of what the layout of my website will look like.

DEVELOPMENT
